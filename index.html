About Us

Founded in 2010, our purpose has always been to protect the ocean environment and sea creatures such as dolphins. We love all kinds of animals in the sea and want them to be able to live without being subjected to the destruction and pollution of their habitats.

We have been working hard to clean the oceans and beaches of trash and other human wastes. We have also saved a great number of sea animals from injuries minor and major.

If you would like to help, you can scroll down to read about the volunteer opportunities or click the button below to donate 5 dollars or more to our cause.

Volunteer Opportunities

If you would like to help our cause, you very much can! We offer volunteer work ranging from cleaning beaches to cleaning oceans. If you want to help clean the ocean, then you can either bring your own scuba-diving gear or borrow one of our own.

Are you interested in working in the vet field? You can even volunteer to help treat sea animals in need. This will benifit your resume greatly.

If you don't want to volunteer but still would like to help, then you can alternatively donate money to our cause.
